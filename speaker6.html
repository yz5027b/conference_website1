Evolution of Visual Comic Art with Technology

Ethan Zuckerman

Ethan Zuckerman (born 1973) is an American media scholar, blogger, and Internet activist. He is the director of the MIT Center for Civic Media, Associate Professor of the Practice in Media Arts and Sciences at MIT and the author of the book "Rewire: Digital Cosmopolitans in the Age of Connection", which won the ZÃ³calo Book Prize

Zuckerman is an Open Society Global Board member, and also sits on the board of directors of Ushahidi, Global Voices and the Ghanaian journalism training nonprofit, PenPlusBytes.

On July 1, 2016, Zuckerman was appointed Associate Professor of the Practice in Media Arts and Sciences at MIT.

He will talk about the digital connection between worldwide comic art and all forms of relevant industries.
